# ServiceDesign - Project for HAMK Design Factory

The DF Service Design Summer Project, undertaken by team Diversified, addresses key challenges 
faced by Sampola, a workshop in HÃ¤meenlinna that employs individuals with disabilities in producing

service design approach, including co-creation, service blueprinting, and iterative testing, we

of service design principles in addressing real-world challenges, thereby empowering Sampola to 
better achieve its social and economic goals.
